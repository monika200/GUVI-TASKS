Mumbai airport's new Covid-19 protocols for international passengers

MUMBAI: The Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport has announced a fresh set of guidelines for all incoming and outgoing international passengers against the backdrop of the new Covid-19 virus strain detected in the UK, an official said here on Tuesday.
As announced on Monday, all flights to and from the UK remain suspended from December 22-31 midnight.

Pollution deaths in India rose to 1.67 million in 2019: Lancet

NEW DELHI:India, whose cities top global pollution lists, faces a growing economic as well as human toll from bad air quality, which was linked to 1.24 million, or 12.5% of total deaths in the previous such study for 2017.

Toxic air killed more people in India in 2019 than in 2017, The Lancet said in a report.

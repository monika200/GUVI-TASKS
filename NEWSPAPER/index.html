Wipro's Rs 9,500 crore buyback offer to open on December 29

NEW DELHI:IT services major Wipro on Tuesday said its up to Rs 9,500-crore share buyback programme will commence December 29 and close on January 11, 2021.
Last month, shareholders had approved Wipro's buyback plan for purchase of up to 23.75 crore equity shares at Rs 400 per share, aggregating to an amount of up to Rs 9,500 crore.

"...the company received final comments from the Securities and Exchange Board of India on December 21, 2020, with respect to the Draft Letter of Offer filed by the company will dispatch the Letter of Offer to eligible shareholders," Wipro said in a regulatory filing.
